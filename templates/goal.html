Our Goal

Through the use of Mindfulness and the 6 Pillars of Giving, we want to create a movement of change that will help improve the well-being and satisfaction of the lives of G-Force Users. Our Diagnostic Giving Quiz will recommend a pillar of giving to focus on and then you will follow a progressive path of challenges, a journey that of self-growth that will leave you more fulfilled and happy!

The Six Pillars

Forgiveness Giving Thanks Give it a Try
Give it a Thought Give it a Rest Care Giving

Below are the in-depth explanations about our Six Pillars of Giving and the reasoning behind their vital importance in a person's life.

Forgiveness

Forgiveness

When we hold on to hurt, pain, resentment, and anger it harms us far more than it harms the offender. It leads to negative thoughts that manifest and keep us in the past. Forgiveness is for our own growth and happiness. It frees us to live in the present.

Giving Thanks

Gratitude helps people feel more positive emotions, relish good experiences, improve their health, deal with adversity, and build strong relationships. Furthermore, research in Positive Psychology has found that people who give thanks are found to be substantially happier and feel more fulfilled!

Giving Thanks

Give it a Try

Give it a Try

It is very beneficial to experience new things by pushing yourself out of your comfort zone to embrace change. By taking on different opportunities to grow, we have the ability to make everyday our masterpiece, improving our emotional and physical well being along with our life-satisfaction

Give it a Thought

Patience is very beneficial. By giving things a thought you are not being reactive and letting your emotions but rather slowing down to understand the situation in the most empathetic and reflective way possible which improves your decision-making and happiness with your future situations.

Give it a Thought

Give it a Rest

Give it a Rest

Giving things a rest means to stop overthinking. By taking a moment to rest and not obsess over the stressors of life you are able to detach yourself from the elements of life that bog you down and live in a more deliberate and self-actualized way.

Care Giving

Care Giving is when people provide support for those around them, whether it be with loved ones, friends, or general acquantiences, there are many ways to care for others. It has been found that people with strong support systems are more successful, healthy, and report better emotional well-beings!
